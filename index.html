About Us

Periphery is an independent games developer based in the UK. We're passionate about the medium, our individual aspirations having long focused around the opportunity to invoke the same human experiences that our predecessors gave to us through games. Although this is a new adventure for us, each member has a professional background from which to draw their experience.

Above all, we seek not to follow in the footsteps of others but to take their lessons and leave our own legacy.

Last Aegis

Command a fleet of spaceships to victory on the fringes of space in this sci-fi inspired tactical role-playing game. Go in fast with squadrons of fighters and outmanoeuvre your foes, all the while providing firing support with your frigates and tearing through hulls with the main batteries from your cruisers - pick them apart by disabling crucial subsystems or just blast them into spacedust! Extensive ship customization enables your own personal playstyle.

Whichever stratagem you choose to employ, know that the fate of your pilots and that of the larger campaign as a whole rests solely on your decisive leadership in an engaging character-driven narrative. Will you rise to the challenge?
